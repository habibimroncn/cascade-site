Cottonwood Select & Better is Ideal for clear Cabinetry, Furniture, and Molding Manufacture. It is also used for clear paint grade applications.

Cottonwood Select & Better includes all FAS2F and Select boards. Select Boards must grade at least a # 1 shop on the back and the face must grade select. This grade is not selected for sap and heartwood and can have a range of colors.
