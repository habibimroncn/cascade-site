Notes

Glossary

Premium admits all boards two board feet and over surface measure, yielding no less than 83.33% sound cuttings. The reverse side will be Frame grade or better.

WANE is limited to 25 percent of the width and 50 percent of the length. Wane may be divided, showing on both board edges.

SIDE BEND limited to 1 inch (25.40 mm).

Face knot size is limited to 0 .5" (12.70 mm) unsound and 1" (25.40 mm) sound in 4 to 5.5" (101.60 to 139.70 mm) widths. In boards exceeding 5.5" (139.70 mm) wide, knot size is limited to 1" (25.40 mm) unsound and, 2" (50.8mm) sound.
